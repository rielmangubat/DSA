About SAIS

The Student Affairs Information System (SAIS) is a digital platform developed to help students and the school administration streamline and improve how student-related matters are handled. It serves as a centralized system where students can file complaints, report incidents, track submissions, and manage their profiles easily.

SAIS promotes transparency, faster response times, and proper documentation of issues concerning student life, discipline, and other affairs. It is designed to create a safer, more responsive, and more organized academic environment.
